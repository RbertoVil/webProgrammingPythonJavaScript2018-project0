About me:

My name is Roberto Villasana, computer engineering student, i was born in Caracas, Venezuela on October 3, 2000. I'm passionate about technology, economy and the videogames almost equally.

Although i was born in Caracas, i'm actually (the month of September of 2020) living in the Venezuelan city of Barinas, i moved to said city in the year 2018 for study reasons. i'm actually studying the 4th semester of "computer engineering" in the (Unellez).

I'm analytical and introverted by nature.
