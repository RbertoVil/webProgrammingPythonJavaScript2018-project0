My passion for technology

The technology, according to "wikipedia" is the sum of techniques, skills, methods and processes used in the production of goods or services or in the accomplishment of objectives, such as scientific investigation. Technology can be the knowledge of their workings.

However when i say that i am passionate about technology, i mean computing, and more specifically, my passion is "software" and "coding". That passion began when i was a kid, when my love for video games and curiosity in how they are created began.
